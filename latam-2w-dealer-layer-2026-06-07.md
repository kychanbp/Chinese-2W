# Dealer Layer — Onboarding & Lead-Buying (2026-06-07)

## Q1 — How to become a dealer (the capital-heavy alternative)

Apply via the brand's dealer-recruitment funnel (form / email / B2B portal). **Barrier = capital, mostly inventory:**

| Brand | Investment | Space | Exclusivity | Apply via |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Shineray (BR)** | **R$450k–R$1M+** tiers (buildout + bike stock) | n/s | exclusive OR multi-brand (checkbox) | shineray.com.br/revendedor |
| **TVS (MX)** | form pivots at **MXN 1.5M** | n/s | n/s | mexico.tvsmotor.com/en/potential-dealer |
| CFMoto/Zontes/QJ/Kove (MX) | gated (declare your own) | "scalable showroom → store" | n/s | brand /distribucion pages |
| Voge (BR) | gated | n/s | pushes **exclusive** | vogebrasil.com.br/contato |
| Hero (CO) | gated | n/s | n/s | heromotos.com.co |
| Bajaj (CO) | gated (B2B portal) | n/s | n/s | via Grupo UMA |

**Reads:** only Shineray/TVS disclose numbers (~USD 80–190k); the dominant cost is **floor-planning bike inventory**, not real estate; value brands tolerate **multi-brand**, premium brands push **exclusivity**. A dealership is the **capital-heavy opposite** of asset-light lead-gen (stock + premises + staff + floor-plan risk + brand approval). Not the path — but the recruitment forms are the *door to the brands*, and dealer economics inform the lead-gen pitch.

## Q2 — Do dealers already pay for leads? YES (validated demand, but a served market)

1. **Dealer referral programs:** Lógica Motos (BR) "Indique e Ganhe" **R$60 PIX/buyer**; CrediOrbe (CO) "Refiere y Gana" on dealer moto sales. OEM voucher promos (Yamaha R$1,000) normalize discount-for-acquisition.
2. **Pay-per-lead is standard:** Webmotors (Plano Controle, plans from **R$137**), iCarros, OLX, Mercado Livre. Moto-specific lead CRMs exist (Followize "CRM para Concessionária de Motos"), packages "from 250 leads/mo." AutoForce: buying leads is accepted practice.
3. **A moto-dealer lead-gen agency sub-industry exists:** **DIVIA** ("Geração de Leads para Concessionárias de Motos," 3,000+ cases), **Motorleads** (multinational BR/MX/CO/CL/EC/AR), Spiner, Vanguarda, B300 (BR), Go Virtual, Adpunto (MX).

## Implication

- ✅ **R1 ("will dealers pay per lead?") is category-validated YES** — dealers already buy leads with a known cost-per-lead benchmark; you sell into an existing budget line, not a cold concept.
- ⚠️ **The space is already served** — entrenched marketplaces (Webmotors/iCarros) + dedicated moto lead-gen agencies (DIVIA, Motorleads) are the dealer's real alternative (sharpens the make-vs-buy / arbitrage-compression finding).
- **Wedge (must differentiate, not "sell leads"):** (1) **finance-qualified leads + loan monetization** — incumbents sell *raw* leads; you sell *finance-ready* ones and earn on the loan too (lets you undercut on the dealer price); (2) the **moto coverage gap** (marketplaces thinner on motos than cars); (3) **speed-to-lead** (~20% fast WhatsApp).
- **Phase-1 to-do:** study DIVIA / Motorleads (pricing, conversion claims) as direct comparables; ask interviewed dealers what they pay Webmotors/agencies per lead today.

## Caveats

Onboarding terms mostly gated (only Shineray/TVS public); dealer-referral examples are individual cases (Lógica Motos), not universal. Mexico dealer-referral for moto *purchases* was not clearly found (Italika programs are fleet/parts). Lead-buying + agency sub-industry are well-corroborated (high confidence).
