# Chinese Two-Wheelers in Latin America — A Lead-Gen Business Investigation

An evidence-based investigation into whether an **asset-light lead-generation business** — selling Chinese motorcycle (ICE + EV) buyers to dealers, lenders, and brands across Latin America — is worth building.

### 👉 Read the decision memo (the entrance): **[kychanbp.github.io/Chinese-2W](https://kychanbp.github.io/Chinese-2W/)**

---

## The verdict (in one paragraph)

LatAm is the best-shaped theater for this idea, but the naive version — *sell cheap leads to dealers* — is **marginal-to-negative** once you use real numbers (the dealer's own ad cost ≈ yours, and ~half of demand is organic). The business is real **only** as a **three-payer play** (dealer fee + loan origination + brand retainer), for a **narrow customer set** (demand-gen-starved challenger brands, not the dominant incumbents), and **only if** a fast, finance-qualified WhatsApp lead actually converts — the one number that can't be settled from a desk. So the recommendation is **not "build" or "drop," but "run a ~$3–5k, 90-day field test that proves or kills it."**

## Method

Built from primary data (UN Comtrade HS 8711, Brazil BCB / Colombia RUNT / Mexico INEGI registration & financing APIs), **parallel multi-agent web research** (brand shares, dealer CAC, challenger momentum across 7 markets), and an explicit **unit-economics model**. Headline figures were independently **fact-checked with ChatGPT Codex** (see the validation ledger) — directionally trustworthy on market structure and growth; the per-lead CAC/conversion inputs are flagged as *hypotheses for the field test*, not audited facts.

## Disclaimer

Decision-support analysis, **not investment advice**. Figures are model outputs on partly-researched, flagged assumptions; several are directional. Do your own diligence before committing capital.
